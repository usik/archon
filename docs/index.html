How it works

From zero to agent team
in three steps

No prompt engineering. No trial and error. Best-in-class engineering cultures, encoded and ready to deploy.

01

Choose a package

Pick zero-to-market-kr, zero-to-market-jp, or zero-to-market-us. One package spins up a full 14-agent team — PM to customer service — with the right harnesses pre-assigned.

02

Connect your LLM and run

Bring your own API key. Archon runs the team. Agents hand off to each other automatically. Every critical decision pauses for your approval.

03

Customize for your team

Advanced users can swap any harness, edit criteria in the UI, or write custom harnesses in YAML. The library grows with your team.
